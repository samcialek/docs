## Why Space Analysis Matters

GRESB requires consumption reported by control type:

| Control | Typical Areas | GRESB Scope |
|---------|---------------|-------------|
| Landlord | Base building, common areas | Scope 1 & 2 |
| Tenant | Leased spaces | Scope 3 |
