## Benchmark Comparison

| Benchmark | Source | Best For | Update Frequency |
|-----------|--------|----------|------------------|
| ENERGY STAR | EPA | Marketing, certification | Annual |
| CBECS | EIA | Statistical analysis | Every 4-5 years |
| ASHRAE 100 | ASHRAE | Engineering targets | Periodic |
| BPD | DOE | Peer comparison | Continuous |
