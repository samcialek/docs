Compliance exposure measures how much of your portfolio is subject to building performance standards and what financial risk that represents.

## Exposure Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Use |
|--------|------------|-----|
| **% of portfolio subject** | GFA under BPS / Total GFA | Scale of exposure |
| **# properties at risk** | Non-compliant count | Priority list |
| **Total penalty exposure** | Sum of projected penalties | Budget planning |
| **Average years to deadline** | Time to act | Urgency assessment |

## Risk Categories

| Status | Definition | Action |
|--------|------------|--------|
| **Compliant** | Meeting current targets | Monitor for future phases |
| **At Risk** | Within 10% of limit | Plan improvements |
| **Non-Compliant** | Exceeding limit | Immediate action needed |
| **Not Subject** | Below size threshold | No action required |

<Tip>
**Investor Reporting:** Compliance exposure is increasingly requested by investors and lenders as part of climate risk disclosure.
</Tip>
