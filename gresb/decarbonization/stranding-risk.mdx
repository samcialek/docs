## Types of Stranding Risk

| Risk Type | Driver | Example |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| **Regulatory** | Building performance laws | Failing BERDO, LL97, BPS |
| **Market** | Tenant/investor preferences | Tenants avoiding high-carbon space |
| **Financial** | Lending requirements | Banks requiring climate risk disclosure |
| **Physical** | Climate impacts | Flood zones, extreme heat |
