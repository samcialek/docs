## GRESB Data Requirements

| Category | What GRESB Asks For | How Abisko Helps |
|----------|---------------------|------------------|
| Energy | Consumption by fuel type, L/T splits | Auto-calculates from meters |
| Emissions | Scope 1, 2, 3; location & market-based | Applies appropriate factors |
| Water | Consumption, coverage % | Tracks water meters |
| Waste | Weight by disposal method | Manual or import |
| Building Certs | LEED, ENERGY STAR labels | Links to ESPM |
