GRESB reporting follows an annual cycle with specific deadlines. Understanding the timeline helps you prepare data and meet submission requirements.

## Typical Annual Timeline

| Month | Activity | Abisko Action |
|-------|----------|---------------|
| **January** | GRESB opens portal | Review prior year data completeness |
| **February-March** | Data collection | Gap-fill, collect tenant data |
| **April** | Portal open for entry | Begin export and upload |
| **May-June** | Submission window | Finalize and submit |
| **July 1** | Deadline (typical) | Final submission |
| **September-October** | Results released | Review scores and feedback |

<Warning>
**Deadline:** The exact deadline varies by year. Check GRESB portal for current year dates. Late submissions may incur penalties or exclusion.
</Warning>

## Pre-Submission Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Data completeness check">
    All properties should have at least 80% data completeness
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Coverage verification">
    Energy and water coverage meet GRESB thresholds
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Landlord/tenant splits">
    All spaces classified with correct control types
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Emissions factors">
    Location and market-based factors configured
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Pre-flight validation">
    Run Abisko's GRESB validation before export
  </Step>
</Steps>
