To sync your properties with Abisko, you need to share them through Energy Star Portfolio Manager. This is a two-step process: adding Abisko as a contact, then sharing your properties.

---

## Step 1: Add Abisko as a Contact

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to ESPM">
    Go to [portfoliomanager.energystar.gov](https://portfoliomanager.energystar.gov) and sign in.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to Contacts">
    Go to [Contacts](https://portfoliomanager.energystar.gov/pm/contact/add?execution=e1s1) in ESPM.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Search for Abisko and click Connect">
    In the search field, type "Abisko" and click **Connect**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/espm/espm-contact-search.png" alt="ESPM contact search showing Abisko" />
</Frame>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Notify Abisko">
    Let Abisko know you've sent a connection request so we can accept it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Share Properties in Bulk

After the connection request is accepted:

### Instructions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Bulk Sharing">
    Navigate to the [Bulk Sharing page](https://portfoliomanager.energystar.gov/pm/sharing/wsBulkSharing) in ESPM.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Abisko">
    From the dropdown labeled "Select web services provider from my contacts book," choose **Abisko ESPM (Abisko)**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select properties">
    Click **Select Properties** and check all the properties you want to see in Abisko.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose access level">
    Select **Bulk Sharing (Simple Option)** and choose **Exchange Data Full Access** (recommended).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Authorize">
    Click **Authorize Exchange**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/espm/espm-bulk-sharing.png" alt="ESPM bulk sharing page with Abisko selected" />
</Frame>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Notify Abisko">
    Let us know you've shared properties so we can accept the shares.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## What Happens Next

Once Abisko accepts your property shares:
- ESPM data syncs automatically overnight
- Abisko configures your portfolio
- You receive access to your properties in Abisko

<Note>
Data syncs every night. Any updates you make in ESPM will appear in Abisko the following day.
</Note>
