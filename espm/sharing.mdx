## Sharing Properties with Abisko

To sync ESPM properties with Abisko, you need to share them through Energy Star Portfolio Manager. This two-step process is described below.

## Step 1: Add Abisko as a Contact

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to ESPM">
    Go to [portfoliomanager.energystar.gov](https://portfoliomanager.energystar.gov) and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Contacts">
    Go to [Contacts](https://portfoliomanager.energystar.gov/pm/contact/add?execution=e1s1) in ESPM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for Abisko and click Connect">
    In the search field, type "Abisko" and click **Connect**.

    ![Espm Connection](/images/espm_connection.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Notify Abisko">
    Let Abisko know you've sent a connection request so we can accept it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Share Properties in Bulk

<Warning>
  This step can only completed after the ESPM Contact request has been accepted by Abisko.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to ESPM Bulk Sharing page">
    Navigate to the [Bulk Sharing page](https://portfoliomanager.energystar.gov/pm/sharing/wsBulkSharing) in ESPM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Abisko">
    Select **Abisko ESPM (Abisko)** in the dropdown labeled "Select web services provider from my contacts book"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select properties">
    Click **Select Properties** and check all the properties you want to share with Abisko.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose access level">
    Select **Bulk Sharing (Simple Option)** and choose **Exchange Data Full Access**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize">
    Click **Authorize Exchange**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Notify Abisko">
    Let Abisko know you've shared properties so we can accept the shares.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  ![ESPM bulk sharing page with Abisko selected](/images/espm/espm-bulk-sharing.png)
</Frame>

---

## Once Abisko accepts your property shares

- ESPM data will begin syncing automatically every night.
- Abisko will configures your portfolio & provide you login credentials.

<Note>
  Data syncs every night. Any updates you make in ESPM will appear in Abisko the following day.
</Note>
