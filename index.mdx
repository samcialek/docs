# Welcome to Abisko

Abisko connects to your Energy Star Portfolio Manager account and transforms property data into the landlord/tenant splits, scope classifications, and compliance insights that GRESB and investors require.

## What Abisko Does

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect ESPM">
    Authorize Abisko to sync your Portfolio Manager properties and meter data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Spaces">
    Define base building vs tenant areas and assign meters to each space.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Data Quality">
    Abisko flags gaps, estimates, and coverage issues before you export.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export for GRESB">
    Download a complete Asset Spreadsheet with all required fields populated.
  </Step>
</Steps>
