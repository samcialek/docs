In Abisko, properties are important places where users manage everything from utility meters and green power purchases to compliance and certification tracking. Users can access different tabs to enter, manage and analyze data for different purposes.

## Property Tabs

The following provides a short description of each property-level tab.

| Tab               | Description                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| ----------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Dashboard         | Performance benchmarks and comparisons (ASHRAE, CBECS, EPA, BPD); annual energy performance and data completeness trends; interactive monthly and annualized plots.                                |
| Annual Trends     | Annual trends for energy, emissions, water, and waste performance and data completeness; lists of completed assessments, certifications, and measures.                                             |
| Performance       | Web-based and PDF performance reports, including: _All-Time Trends Report_, _Monthly Performance Trends Report_, _Monthly Cost Trends Report_, _Annualized ESPM Trends Report_, and _PARC Report_. |
| Data Quality      | Property and meter-level summaries of data quality and completeness, including detailed lists of all detected gaps, overlaps, and jumps for each meter.                                            |
| Compliance        | Tracking of benchmarking, BPS, and audit policy exposure and submissions; penalty tracking across policies; BPS compliance exposure analysis and decarbonization planning.                         |
| GRESB             | GRESB asset-level spreadsheet data collection, validation, and tracking for property- and meter-level data.                                                                                        |
| CRREM             | CRREM-based energy and emissions transition risk analysis and decarbonization planning.                                                                                                            |
| Green Power       | Tracking of green power purchases (e.g., offsite RECs) and onsite solar PV systems.                                                                                                                |
| PARC              | Tracking of certifications, ratings, assessments, measures and HVAC systems                                                                                                                        |
| Spaces            | Breakdown of energy & emissions performance by space type (e.g. tenant vs. landlord).                                                                                                              |
| Meter Exploration | Interactive dashboards for exploring performance trends across different combinations of meters.                                                                                                   |
| Meters            | Management of all energy, water and waste meters associated with the property.                                                                                                                     |
| Climate Risk      | Optional add-on for assessing climate risk across multiple categories, including _heat, precipitation, drought, fire, and flood._                                                                  |
| Files             | Centralized location to upload, store, and manage files associated with the property.                                                                                                              |
