Property meters are used to track all energy, water and waste consumption data. This data is used to for calculating and reporting property and portfolio-level performance metrics. Meter data can come from a variety of sources and can be enriched to enable more complex reporting.

## Meter Data Sources

Meter data can come from a variety of sources.

1. Abisko
   - Create and manage all energy, water and waste meters and consumption data directly on the platform. [Learn More](/properties-meters/meters/create-edit-meters).
2. ENERGY STAR Portfolio ManagerÂ® (ESPM)
   - Leverage a comprehensive integration with ESPM to sync all meters and consumption data tracked nightly or on-demand. [Learn More](/espm/compatibility).
3. Utility Sync
   - Leverage Abisko's utility sync capabilities to automatically import meter data from your utility on a quarterly basis.
