## To edit or delete a meter in Abisko

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Property → Meters → Energy / Water / Waste">
    
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hover the mouse over a row in the table for the meter you would like to edit">
    
  </Step>
  <Step title="3.	Click the icon to the right of the row that shows itself while hovering">
    - Click the edit icon to open the sidebar to edit
    - Click the trash icon to delete the meter after confirmation
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  For properties that are synced from ESPM, meters must be edited or deleted directly in ESPM. The updates will be reflected after the nightly sync.
</Note>
