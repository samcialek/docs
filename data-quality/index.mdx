## Data Quality Dashboard

Abisko's Data Quality dashboard gives you a portfolio-wide view of issues:

| Metric | Target | Action if Below |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Completeness | 80%+ | Gap-fill or collect data |
| Coverage | 80%+ | Add meters or estimates |
| Gaps | 0 | Review and fill |
| Overlaps | 0 | Resolve duplicates |
| Jumps | Review | Verify or correct |

<Warning>
**GRESB Impact:** Properties with significant data quality issues may be excluded from performance scoring or flagged during validation.
</Warning>
